Rubik's Cubes

One of my favorite hobbies is to collect and solve twisty puzzles (twisty puzzles are puzzles that are similar to a Rubik's cube). I bought and solved my first Rubik's cube around 2014, and I have been hooked on them ever since. I enjoy speedcubing, although I have only been to one competition (you can see my results here). Of course, there is also some interesting math going on when you apply group theory to the Rubik's cube (you can find out some about that here)!

I plan to add pictures of my puzzles here, so stay tuned for those!
